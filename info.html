Info

Ireland may be a great place for surfing, but it is important to have the right equipment as the atlantic sea can be very cold / etc throughout the year.



Every serious surfer should own :
  • Suitable wetsuit depending on weather
  • Neoprene gloves
  • Neoprene/Surfing boots or socks


It's also necessary to have :
  1. Appropiate surfboard based on preference
  2. Proper leash / make sure it is not damaged
  3. Wax board if necessary - improves grip
